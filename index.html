Grammy's House

Info

My grandmother, Stephania Hassan —neé Stephanie Louise Richardson— (b. April 14, 1947) is an artist and retired social worker; small business owner; and teacher. I, Kyle Richardson, compiled the photos and printed ephemera on this site from a box she gave me prior to her move to Egypt in 2018. Grammy's house is an ode to home, to her, and the city that raised us both. Use the floorplan to navigate around the collection.
