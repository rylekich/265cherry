You are looking out of Stephania’s apartment window.¹ She lived on the 25th floor at 265 Cherry Street² between 1979 and 2004.

¹ The view towards the Financial District ceased to exist after Extell Development Company demolished the neighborhood supermarket in 2014 and finished erecting the luxury housing development in 2019.

² Lands End II is a two 26-story tower building complex at 265-275 Cherry Street (near Rutgers Slip) that was built in 1979 as part of the Two Bridges Urban Renewal Area. The low and middle income development was New York City’s first Section 8 project. L+M Development Partners and CIM Group partnered in 2013 to purchase Lands End II for $279 Million.

Stephania Hassan (neé Stephanie Richardson) is an artist, former social worker, and retired special education teacher from New York City. She is my grandmother and took these beautiful photos. Website designed and built by Kyle Richardson in 2020.
